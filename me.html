Me

So this is where I introduce myself and why this page exists.


I am Australian, but live in London, England. I like geeking out with computers and mobile technology but also being outdoors and being physical. I am an engineer but not of the software kind, though maybe one day I might be.

This website launches my foray into the digital world and aims to be a work in progress. I feel like technology is moving along at such a break neck speed now, that everyone should know a little about coding, the interwebs and the new internet of things that we are abou to find ourselves in.

I called the website sprckt because I love cycling and also love using the simple gear icon on apps. It is full on geek! Hopefully there will be an element of the site that ends up being part travelogue as well, but hey lets not get too ahead of ourselves.

This website is primarily for me, but if you find anything of use in here, then please feel free to use it

Eric 10.06.2015
